Facility

  • The department has three labs.
  • Computers with advanced Quad-Core AMD (3.6 GHz) processors are used by students and faculties to perform basic programming to parallel processing.
  • All the computers are equipped with software tools / IDEs to support program development.
  • Final year students work on projects in groups or individuals according to syllabus of GTU.
  • The project work is divided in two phases which are undertaken by students in 7th and 8th semester. Current projects undertaken by students comprise of API development in php, using Facebook API, php programming and Android application development.
  • Creative workshops like Ethical Hacking, are organized every year to expose students to real world applications.
